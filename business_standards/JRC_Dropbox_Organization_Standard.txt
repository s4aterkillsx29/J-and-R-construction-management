J & R Construction Dropbox organization standard

One-root rule
- Use one selected Dropbox business folder as the single active J&R Dropbox root.
- Do not create multiple Dropbox business roots.
- Do not create multiple invoice/document roots.
- All J&R working files, job files, invoices, estimates, receipts, photos, logs, backups, imports, exports, and standards belong under the one selected Dropbox business root.

Legacy candidate rule
- Dropbox/Invoices2026 1.0, Dropbox/J and R Construction, and Dropbox/JRC are legacy/candidate locations only.
- If they already exist, inspect/index them to avoid losing records.
- Do not recreate all of them.
- Do not leave all of them active as separate businesses.
- Choose one as the configured Dropbox business root, then organize everything under that one root.
- OneDrive/Desktop, Documents/JRC, and C:/JRC are legacy/local candidates for review or one-time import only, not alternate active business roots.

Safe repair rule
- Recreate missing folders only.
- Add readme/index files only when needed.
- Do not delete, move, overwrite, rename, archive, or merge existing Dropbox files unless the owner explicitly asks.
- Preserve existing job files, invoices, estimates, receipts, photos, logs, exports, backups, ChatGPT imports, and standards.
