CUSTOMER INVOICE / ESTIMATE — REQUIRED SECTIONS
-----------------------------------------------
  A. Header block (J&R, phone, date, job code, invoice # if used)
  B. Bill to (customer name, address)
  C. Job site (address, lot/parcel if known)
  D. SCOPE OF WORK — summary paragraph + item/description table
  E. PRICING table:
       Standard value ........................ $XX,XXX.XX   (omit if no discount)
       Close family & friends discount ....... -$X,XXX.XX  (family jobs only)
       YOUR PRICE ............................ $XX,XXX.XX
  F. PAYMENT TERMS (see below)
  G. Scope notes / exclusions
  H. Thank you line

  Family/friends jobs: ALWAYS show standard value and discount as separate lines.
  Internal margin, tax set-aside, owner draw: INTERNAL WORKUP ONLY — never on customer PDF.

PAYMENT TERMS
-------------
  Standard jobs (default):
    50% deposit before work | 50% balance on completion

  Material + labor heavy jobs (Jacob preference — e.g. JRC-315 fence):
    70% deposit before materials purchase and labor scheduling
    30% balance upon completion after walkthrough

  Jacob may override per job — log the override in NEW_JOB_FINANCIAL_SETUP.txt.

INTERNAL WORKUP — REQUIRED SECTIONS
-----------------------------------
  A. Header: INTERNAL — CONFIDENTIAL
  B. Customer / site / layout source
  C. FINAL RESULT NUMBERS table (always include):
       Standard retail value (if discount job)
       Close family & friends discount (if applicable)
       Customer quote (draft or sent status)
       Deposit % / Balance % with dollar amounts
       Materials + rental
       Labor (days + helper + $50 OH per instance)
       Site difficulty / add-ons
       TOTAL INTERNAL COST (highlight row)
       Tax set-aside when paid (7.35% + catch-up share)
       Business margin ($ and %)
       Owner draw (job costing only — separate from margin)
       COUNT AS INCOME? (yes/no + rule)
  D. Scope of work (detail)
  E. Timeline table (day / work)
