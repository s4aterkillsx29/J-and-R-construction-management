J & R Construction log workflow standard

When the owner says "log" or asks to log an update:
- Create a dated business log entry summarizing the update.
- Include enough job, customer, address, document, payment, or source context to find the entry later.
- Confirm back what was logged.
- Refresh the latest business log mirror file: JRC_Business_Log_Latest.txt.
- Copy the latest business log mirror into the configured local Dropbox business folder when Dropbox is set.
- If Dropbox is not configured or unavailable, say so clearly and keep the export mirror updated.

App behavior:
- Database.log writes to the business_log table.
- Database.log also refreshes exports/JRC_Business_Log_Latest.txt.
- If app setting dropbox_folder is configured, Database.log copies that mirror file into the Dropbox folder.
