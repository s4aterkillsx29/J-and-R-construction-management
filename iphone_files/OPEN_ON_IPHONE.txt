J & R Construction - iPhone Files
================================

Find invoices on your iPhone:
1. Open the Files app.
2. Tap Browse.
3. If you use Dropbox with J&R: Dropbox > your J&R folder > iphone_files > Invoices.
4. If you use iCloud Drive: iCloud Drive > J&R Construction > iphone_files > Invoices.
5. If you use the J&R mobile host: open Mobile > Files on your phone browser.
