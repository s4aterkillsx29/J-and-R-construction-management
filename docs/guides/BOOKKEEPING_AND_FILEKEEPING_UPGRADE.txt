J and R Construction Manager v4.0 - Bookkeeping and Filekeeping Upgrade

Added:
- Bookkeeping Control Center at /bookkeeping.
- Reconcile job income, expenses, worker/helper pay, worker cost fees, receipts, and evidence into a ledger.
- Export JRC bookkeeping ledger CSV for tax review.
- Missing receipt count, receivables, estimated profit, duplicate-file warnings, and reconciliation run history.
- Bookkeeping rules for J&R categories like materials, worker pay, vehicle/truck, insurance/admin, tools/equipment, and income/payments.
- System Check auto-repairs bookkeeping tables on older installs.

Security kept:
- Remembered-device cookies are random tokens only.
- Database stores only cookie fingerprints, not raw cookie values.
- HttpOnly + SameSite=Strict are used.
- Secure cookies are used in HTTPS/public-host mode; local LAN HTTP uses browser-compatible cookies with server-side hashing and audit logs.
- No hidden backdoor/no-footprint access is included. Owner recovery is visible, local-host-only, and logged.

Use:
1. Install/update normally.
2. Open J and R Construction Manager.
3. Run System Check.
4. Open Bookkeeping.
5. Click Reconcile Bookkeeping Now.
6. Export CSV when needed for tax/bookkeeping review.
