J&R Construction Manager v5.2 - Customer Portal + Job Requests

This update adds a customer account classification and customer-only dashboard.

Customer users can:
- log in to a customer dashboard
- create job requests from /customer/request
- review their own requests at /customer/requests
- use a customer-safe mobile portal

Customer users cannot see:
- internal J&R job costing
- payroll or worker pay
- expenses/bookkeeping/tax records
- full file explorer
- other customers
- admin, hosting, devices, or program settings

Owner/admin review:
- Admin/manager users with application-management permission can review customer job requests at /customers/requests.
- Internal notes are hidden from customers.
- Customer-visible notes can be posted for the customer dashboard.

Installer / login behavior:
- The installer still preserves business data and does not require every user to install the desktop program.
- The web login automatically sends users to the dashboard that matches their account role.
- Customer users get the customer dashboard. Admins get the full business dashboard.

Security model:
- Customer accounts are separate from worker/company accounts.
- Customers do not automatically create internal jobs or prices. Requests remain pending until owner/admin review.
