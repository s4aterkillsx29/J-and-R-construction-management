JRC Construction Manager v4.6 - Full QA and Business Readiness

This release adds a deeper offline Full QA Test. It checks required files, folders, Python syntax, database integrity, key tables, route definitions, security patterns, account/application features, payroll/job cost features, filekeeping/bookkeeping features, installer safety, and documentation.

Use after install:
1. Open J and R Construction Manager.
2. Click System Check.
3. Click Full QA Test.
4. Review the generated report in exports.

Important: this automated QA cannot replace manually clicking every screen on your Windows PC, but it catches the common structural, syntax, database, route, and packaging issues before daily business use.
