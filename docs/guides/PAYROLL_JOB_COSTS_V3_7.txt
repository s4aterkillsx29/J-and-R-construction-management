J and R Construction Manager v3.7 - Payroll + Job Cost Automation

New features:
- Payroll Center for logging worker/helper pay by job.
- Optional $100 worker cost fee tracking per payroll entry.
- Payroll entries automatically feed job cost totals.
- Job Cost Dashboard totals job price, paid amount, balance, expenses, worker pay, worker fees, and estimated profit.
- Payroll CSV and Job Cost CSV exports.
- Auto-repair creates/updates payroll/accounting tables for older installs.
- Role permissions: admin/manager can manage payroll; workers/viewers remain read-only.

Important: This is business record software, not a certified payroll/tax filing service. Use a CPA/tax professional for final payroll classification, 1099/W-2, withholding, and tax filing decisions.
