J and R Construction Manager v3.7 Payroll + Job Cost Automation

This update adds an advanced Payroll Center and Job Cost Dashboard for J & R Construction.

Key features:
- Log worker/helper payroll by worker, job, date, hours, rate, amount, payment method, and status.
- Track optional worker cost fees separately from base pay.
- Payroll entries automatically feed job cost totals.
- Job Cost Dashboard compares job price, paid amount, balance due, expenses, worker pay, worker fees, total known cost, and estimated profit.
- Export payroll and job cost CSV reports.
- Auto-repair creates and updates payroll/accounting tables for older installs.
- Role protection keeps worker/viewer accounts read-only while admin/manager can manage payroll.

Security/permissions:
- Admin and manager roles can manage payroll and costs.
- Worker/viewer roles remain read-only by default.
- Passwords remain hashed in the database.
- Admin can still approve/deny account requests and revoke sessions.

Important:
This program is business record software, not a certified payroll service, CPA, or tax filing system. Use a tax professional for final payroll classification, withholding, 1099/W-2, and tax filings.
