Run on your PC (with Dropbox Desktop syncing):

1. Open J&R Job Manager → Dropbox / Remote
2. Set your real Dropbox business folder (e.g. Dropbox/J and R Construction)
3. Click Save
4. Click Reorganize Dropbox Business

Or from terminal in the program folder:

    python tools/reorganize_dropbox_business.py

On iPhone after sync:
Files app → Dropbox → your J&R folder → START_HERE_NAVIGATION.txt

Nothing is deleted. A full snapshot is saved to 09_Archive/_PreReorg_Snapshot_* first.
