J & R CONSTRUCTION — LOGGING STANDARDS (OFFICE ASSISTANT + PHONE CURSOR)
Updated: 2026-06-29

Every business log entry should include:
  - Date and time
  - Job name or address (e.g. 315 Lily Sassafras)
  - Who (owner, helper, customer contact if relevant)
  - What happened (work done, materials, payment, issue)
  - Next step (if any)

PHONE / FIELD LOGS (Dropbox):
  - Write to the job folder or 00_START_HERE field notes
  - Dropbox syncs to office PC — do not use GitHub for logs
