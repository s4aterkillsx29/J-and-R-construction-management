J & R CONSTRUCTION — LOGGING STANDARDS (OFFICE ASSISTANT + PHONE CURSOR)
Updated: 2026-07-04

Every business log entry should include:
  - Date and time
  - Job name or address (e.g. 315 Lily Sassafras)
  - Who (owner, helper, customer contact if relevant)
  - What happened (work done, materials, payment, issue)
  - Next step (if any)

PHONE / FIELD LOGS (Dropbox):
  - Write to the job folder or 00_START_HERE field notes
  - Dropbox syncs to office PC — do not use GitHub for logs

DESKTOP AFTER PHONE SESSION:
  1. scripts\Sync-JRCBusinessFolders.ps1
  2. scripts\Refresh-ReadableBusinessReports.ps1
  3. Construction Manager → Office Records Sync
  4. Tell desktop Cursor: "log"

CURSOR / CLOUD AGENT — WHEN JACOB SAYS "LOG":
  Logging is NOT complete until sync runs. Always do BOTH:
  1. Update office CSVs + field logs (scripts/templates/dropbox_workspace/)
  2. Run: python -m app.dropbox_workspace --sync
     → pushes templates to dropbox-business mirror + Office Records Sync to DB
  3. Commit + push git templates (app code path)
  On office PC: Dropbox Desktop picks up mirror/synced files after Sync-JRCBusinessFolders.ps1

CUSTOMER vs INTERNAL:
  - Customer quotes/invoices: no internal labor rates or helper pay
  - Internal workups: mark INTERNAL USE ONLY

OWNER DRAWS (PAID MYSELF):
  - When Jacob pays himself from business checking, log as OWNER DRAW — not helper pay, not expense
  - Field half day (owner): $120 | Field full day (owner): $240 | Business/office day: $170
  - Business/office day = e.g. Wayne staining while owner on business day ($170)
  - Office CSV: dropbox-records/06_bookkeeping/Owner_Draws_Register.csv
  - Program: Construction Manager -> Owner Draws, or tell Cursor: "paid myself $___ today for ___"
  - Owner draws are equity distributions for a sole proprietor (not deductible wages)

PERSONAL FINANCES (JACOB — NOT BUSINESS):
  - Personal bills, personal checking, personal truck insurance → 07_Personal_Finances/
  - Office CSVs: Personal_Expenses_Register.csv, Personal_Income_Register.csv, Personal_Account_Balances.csv
  - Owner draw transfers TO personal are logged in BOTH business Owner_Draws_Register AND Personal_Income_Register
  - Personal bills are NOT business expenses
