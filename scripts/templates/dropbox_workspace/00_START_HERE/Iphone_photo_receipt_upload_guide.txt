Use this with phone Cursor + Dropbox. Business receipts NEVER go to GitHub.

================================================================================
QUICK STEPS (iPhone)
================================================================================

1. Take photo of receipt (or save PDF/screenshot).

2. Save/share to Dropbox → your ONE workspace:
   JRC_COMPLETE_BUSINESS_FOLDER_2026-06-22 (inner folder)

3. Drop the file into:
   02_RECEIPTS_PHOTO_INBOX/
   (See Readme_drop_photos_here.txt in that folder.)

4. OPTIONAL — rename before sync (helps auto-filing):
   YYYY-MM-DD_JobAddress_Supplier_Type_Amount.jpg
   Example: 2026-06-29_315Lily_GarrisEvans_lumber_127.45.jpg

5. Wait for Dropbox to sync (Wi-Fi or cellular).

6. On PC (evening): Log / Sync — or tell desktop Cursor "log sync".

7. Send receipt photos to desktop Cursor for careful analysis — triple-check
   amounts, job, supplier, and lumber line items before final filing.
